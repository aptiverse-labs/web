# Aptiverse — paid-ad copy variants

Five variants written to four angles. Each fits Meta Ads (Facebook /
Instagram) and LinkedIn limits. Headline + Primary text + Description
+ CTA per variant.

**Meta Ads limits** (under which everything stays readable without
"see more" truncation on mobile):
- Headline: 27 characters
- Primary text: 125 characters
- Description: 27 characters

**LinkedIn Sponsored Content limits** (used the LinkedIn-friendly
expanded primary text below each variant):
- Headline: 70 characters
- Intro: 600 characters but 150 ideal for engagement

All variants comply with the no-vendor-names rule — no "Claude",
"Anthropic", "Paystack", "Stripe" surfaces.

## Notes on targeting

**Meta Ads — audience suggestions:**
- Parents (25-55), South Africa, interests: NSFAS, Funza Lushaka, UCT/Wits/Stellenbosch, "matric tutoring", "private school". Layer with parent-of-children-13-17 lookalike.
- Learners (16-19), South Africa, interests: NSC past papers, school accounts on TikTok (Cape Town College etc.), gaming overlap.

**LinkedIn — audience suggestions:**
- Founders, school principals, education NGO leaders.
- Job titles: Head of School, Academic Director.
- Industries: Education Management, Primary/Secondary Education.

**Visuals to pair with each:**
- V1 (parent angle): mastery forecast scene (the 75% chart drawing up)
- V2 (curriculum-aware): tutor chat scene (citation chip lands)
- V3 (calmer matric): closing shots from the 90s cut — sunset + parent texting
- V4 (SBA Coach): SBA Coach scene (annotations bloom in)
- V5 (tutors): record a Tutor view if/when one is built; for now use the past-paper walk-through scene

## A/B priorities

Run V1 and V2 first — they're the two most-tested SaaS angles
(outcome vs. differentiation). Whichever pulls the lower CPL after
2-3 days gets scaled; the loser gets retired or recombined. V3 is the
retargeting variant for site visitors who didn't convert. V4 is
seasonal (April-September peak). V5 is its own funnel — keep
separate.
